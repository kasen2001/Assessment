How We Can Help

Our support team can assist with common ticket booking questions before and after you reserve your place.

    Get help choosing an event, ticket type, or quantity.
  • Account Help
    Ask about login, registration, and customer details.

Contact Support

Send a message and the support team will review your request.

Common Questions

How do I book a ticket?

Go to an event's detail page, select the quantity, and click Book Now. You must be logged in.

Can I change my ticket?

Contact support with your event name and booking details so the team can review your request.

Where can I find events?

Use the Home page to browse and search for upcoming music festival events.
